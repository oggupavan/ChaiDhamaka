Our Story

Brewing Perfect Cup of CHAI Is An Art

Chai Dhamaka is a popular beverage chain that offers a wide variety of hot and cold beverages to its customers. The chain is known for its unique blend of flavors and high-quality ingredients, which are carefully selected to create a memorable drinking experience.
One of the most popular offerings at Chai Dhamaka is its hot chai, which is a spiced tea beverage that has its roots in Indian culture.
The chai is made with a blend of black tea, milk, and a combination of spices, including cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves. The result is a warming and comforting drink that is perfect for sipping on a cold day.
